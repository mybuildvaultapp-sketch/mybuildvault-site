Everything You Need to Document Your Build

From bolt-ons to full engine swaps, Build Vault keeps your entire project organized in one place.

Track Every Mod

Log modifications with part numbers, vendors, install dates, and costs. Add before-and-after photos to document the transformation.

Monitor Your Investment

See exactly how much you've invested—broken down by category. Finally answer "how much have I spent on this thing?" with actual numbers.

Generate Build Sheets

Create professional PDFs for car shows, insurance claims, or when it's time to sell. Show buyers exactly what's been done.

VIN Decode Your Specs

Enter your VIN and let the app pull factory specs—stock horsepower, torque, weight, drivetrain. Know exactly where you started.

Log Your Dyno Runs

Made some power? Record it. Track dyno results, drag times, boost levels, and weight over time. Compare your gains to stock.

Never Lose a Document

Store registrations, titles, insurance cards, invoices, and service records. Get alerts before documents expire.
